== Description == 

Creates animated Go Top/Go Down buttons in the right bottom corner of your blog. After click, scrolls to top or bottom of the page.

Uses jQuery for setting transparency when you move your mouse over the button.
 
== Installation == 

To install: 
 

1. Drop the 'wd3k-go-top-down' folder into your 'wp-content/plugins' folder
 
2. Go to your Administration: Plugins page and activate the "WD3k Go Top Down" plugin

3. Configuration: (No config needed now)
